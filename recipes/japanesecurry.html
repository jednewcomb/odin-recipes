Japanese Curry

Description

A simple and poignant dish which will keep you coming back.

This Japanese-style curry is a healthy choice for people of all ages

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless bonless chicken thighs
  • 1 tablespoon olie oil
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper
  • 5 1/3 cups water, divided
  • 1 package S&B Golden Curry blocks
  • 1 can peas, drained
  • 5 potatoes, halved and skinned
  • 1 package sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cups jasmine rice

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in large skillet after seasoning
  2. After 3-4 minutes each side of chicken, add vegetables
  3. Add water and bring to a boil, once at a boil, turn heat to low for simmer
  4. Let simmer 15 minutes, then turn heat off
  5. Add curry blocks and stir thoroughly for 2-3 minutes
  6. Serve on a bed of jasmine rice once consistency is to your liking.
