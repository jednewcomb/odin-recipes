World's Best Lasagna

Description

Wow your S/O's parents with this delicious array of cascading cheeses and meats between perfectly fluffy and seasoned pasta blankets.

Their parents will be scheduling the wedding before they've even finished their plate of the World's Best Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes, TomatoSauce, Tomato Paste
  • 2 Tablespoons White Sugar
  • Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasonings
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Create a ricotta mixture
  4. Layer
  5. Cover with foil and Bake
  6. Allow Lasagna to rest before serving
Layering
  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Cheese Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan
  7. Repeat, topping with Parmesan
